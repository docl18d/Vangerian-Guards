Curse Forge is a website that contains all the AddOns you could ever want to make your time playing easier. Not just for WOW but for many live Multi player online games that Curse Forge Supports.

What is an AddOn?

An AddOn is a program that is dowloaded into your WOW Interface folder on your computer in the addon section.

They are programs that help you find things, find groups, fight things, fabricate things, and get funding for things.

Curse Forge Access

  1. Go to https://www.curseforge.com/
  2. Click the 'Login' button and set up a free account
  3. Once done click the orange 'Get Curseforge' App Button. Curse Forge must be installed on your computer so your info isn't on the web.
  4. Make a desktop Icon on your desktop or in a folder where you keep you WOW Log In programs (i.e Battle Net). This will ensure the programs are in the right location. You can also use the desktop app to uniusnstall the AddOns if you no longer want them
  5. It will ask you to choose a game. Click on WOW Retail. You can also get AddOns for WOW Classic and WoTLK here if you play that...I don't know why you would though.
  6. Use the Search Button to find the AddOns you need.
  7. When you find the AddOn just click the 'Install' button
  8. Use the 'My AddOns' Button to display all the addons you have downloaded and their update status.
  9. Each week after the weekly WOW update on Tuesday, go to the Curse Forge App to the 'My AddOns' tab.
  10. Hit the "Update All' button in your 'My Addons' tab. It will aoutmatically update all the out of date AddOns.

The following addons are needed for Dungeons and Raids

MaxDps Rotation Helper

  • an addon that helps tell you when and what spell or ability you should be casting next.
  • It puts a small white circle around the spell or ability icon which are on your task bars.

Deadly Boss Mobs (DBM)

  • This is the core program that provides combat warnings, timers, and infoframes for 5 man dungeons and raids.
  • This is a must have. One of the most popular AddOns.

Deadly Boss Mobs (DBM)-Dungeons

  • This is the specific package for DBM Dungeons.
  • There are additional DBM packages for other area and settings such as voice or archeology professions.
  • Just type DBM in Curse Forge search and they will all show up.
  • Add the ones you think you will need.

DBM-Spell Timers

  • Helpful package of DBM to give you spell or buff timers.

Details! Damage Meter

  • Allows you to see what damage or healing you are doing during any encounter.
  • Very useful

GTFO

  • GTFO provides an audible alert when you're standing in something you're not supposed to be standing in.
  • In some cases, you'll be warned before you start taking damage.
  • This mod improves your situational awareness and is recommended for dungeon divers and raiders of all skill levels as even the most seasoned veteran sometimes needs a reminder to GTFO.

MaxDPS (whatever your class is)

  • There is a MaxDPS for each class, i.e. MaxDPS Hunter.

  • It Highlights your spells or abilities that should be played.
  • Very useful when learning a new class

NPC Scan

  • NPCScan helps you find NPCs by scanning nameplates, minimap vignettes, and mouseover targets, with an optional dynamic targeting keybinding.
  • Once an NPC is found an audio alert is played and the location and Icon is desplayed on the map

Weak Auras

Raider.IO Mythic Plus & Raiding

  • When cued for an instance it allows the raid leader to see your profession and your ranking to pick or not pick you based on instance requirements.

    The following add ons are good for for Banking and Auction House

    Auctionator

    • This is the AH GUI that I use instead of the basic WOW frames.
    • It does a lot and this is one Addon you definitely need.

    Scrap (Junk Seller)

    • Scrap sells all your junk when you visit a merchant, but not just grays.
    • It comes with multiple (optional) intelligent junk filters, and can even memorize which items you normally sell.
    • More importantly, it is designed to be very easy to configure which items to sell and tags junk items in the inventory accordingly.
    • It can also automatically repair your equipment each time you open up a vendor window

    Bagnon

    • I love this addon
    • Bagnon is the most popular replacement of the bag interface, designed to help the player find items as quickly and easily as possible
    • It organizes all bags into a single window, highlights important items, sorts on request and helps you find them using a search engine.
